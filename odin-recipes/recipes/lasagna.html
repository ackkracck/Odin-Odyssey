Classic Baked Lasagna

A warm, cheesy lasagna baked to perfection — perfect for family dinners or cozy nights in.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 700ml tomato passata
  • 9–12 lasagna sheets
  • 300g mozzarella, shredded
  • 250g ricotta
  • Fresh basil, salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Sauté onion and beef until browned.
  2. Stir in passata and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Layer sauce, pasta, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes until golden.
"The secret to great lasagna is patience — let it rest before cutting."
